Meet VALKANIA, a Balkan Rhythm'n'Blues Band from London

Valkania / YouTube
The name: Valkania comes from the Greek word Βαλκάνια, meaning simply - the Balkans; it has a nice female touch to it; nothing to do with volcanoes! Residency The residency of the band is North East London Mission (style): The band was formed in 2009 with the aim to marry Balkan with Western music Not a folk band that plays traditional music.
